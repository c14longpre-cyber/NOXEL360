# NOXEL SEO

## 1) 기능 헤더
**약속:** 지능적 최적화를 통해 검색 가시성, 순위 및 유기적 성장을 극대화합니다.

## 2) 이 모듈이 존재하는 이유
1. SEO tools are fragmented and overly technical.
2. Businesses struggle to understand *what to fix first*.
3. NOXEL360 requires a unified, intelligent SEO engine connected to real data.

**핵심 문제:**
SEO is complex, slow, and unclear — most tools provide data, but not direction.

**주요 사용자:**
- Growth teams scaling organic traffic
- Developers optimizing technical SEO
- Businesses seeking predictable visibility and acquisition

## 3) 모듈 기능 (시간이 지나도 안정적)

### A. 수집 / 입력
- Crawls websites and pages
- Detects SEO issues (technical, content, structure)
- Integrates keywords, search data, and performance metrics

### B. 분석 / 이해
- Scores website health and SEO performance
- Identifies critical vs minor issues
- Understands ranking factors and content gaps

### C. 우선순위 / 행동
- Recommends high-impact SEO actions first
- Guides fixes with clear explanations
- Supports automation of optimization tasks

### D. 모니터링 / 비교 (장기 비전)
- Tracks rankings, traffic, and SEO improvements over time
- Measures impact of changes
- Evolves into a predictive SEO engine

## 4) NOXEL360 내 연결
- **NOXEL ANALYTICS:** Measures traffic and performance impact
- **NOXEL LINKS:** Improves internal and external link structure
- **NOXEL OPTIMA:** Enhances performance for better rankings
- **NOXEL MAESTRO:** Coordinates SEO actions across modules
