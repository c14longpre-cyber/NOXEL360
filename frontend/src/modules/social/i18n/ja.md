# NOXEL SOCIAL

## 1) 機能ヘッダー
**約束:** インテリジェントなコンテンツとオーディエンス分析でソーシャルプレゼンスを管理・成長させます。

## 2) このモジュールが存在する理由
1. Social platforms are disconnected from core business data.
2. Content strategies lack clarity and performance feedback.
3. NOXEL360 requires a unified social intelligence layer.

**中核的問題:**
Social media generates data and attention — but lacks structured intelligence and integration.

**主なユーザー:**
- Content creators and social managers
- Growth teams scaling audience reach
- Businesses building brand presence

## 3) モジュール機能（時間的に安定）

### A. 収集 / 入力
- Aggregates data from social platforms
- Tracks posts, engagement, and audience metrics
- Connects social accounts and content pipelines

### B. 分析 / 理解
- Identifies high-performing content patterns
- Analyzes audience behavior and engagement
- Detects trends and content opportunities

### C. 優先順位 / アクション
- Recommends content strategies and posting schedules
- Suggests improvements for engagement and reach
- Supports automation of publishing workflows

### D. 監視 / 比較（長期ビジョン）
- Tracks growth, engagement, and conversions over time
- Compares performance across platforms
- Evolves into an intelligent content engine

## 4) NOXEL360内の接続
- **NOXEL ANALYTICS:** Measures traffic and engagement impact
- **NOXEL CRM:** Connects audience to customer lifecycle
- **NOXEL NEXUS:** Adapts content by language and culture
- **NOXEL MAESTRO:** Coordinates social strategies across modules
